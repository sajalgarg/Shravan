Special Education & Vocational training
Special Education :

Children with mental retardation and associated disabilities are assessed by the qualified Special Educators in order to find out the current level of functioning in various skill areas such as self help skills, functional academics and concept formation. Accordingly Individualized Educational Programmes are developed for each child. Involvement of parents are encouraged in all stages of implementation of IEP & other activities.

Vocational Training :

These services aim at providing adequate support to adults with mental retardation and/or associated disabilities in leading an independent life. The skilis training includes assess ment of generic skills and job specific skills followed by drawing of a detailed vocational profile in order to identify training needs and to prepare the vocational training programme accordingly .

Library and Documentation Services

Institute has a well- equipped library to cater the information requirements of staff, student, trainees, professional working with institute. Students, professionals and doctors from other institutions are also encouraged to avail the facilities by becoming reference members. The main objective of our is to make library as a state reference and resource centre in the field of the mental retardation. At present, the library is in initial stage and having strength of 800 reference books and 5 leading International as well as national periodical, Journals in the field of Psychology, Special Education and disability rehabilitation. The library also subscribes 05 English & Hindi Newspaper and employment News for the readers. Working hours of library is from 9 A.M. to 4 P.M. on all working day

Physiotherapy
Physiotherapy Services

These services have been provided with an objective to provide physical therapy for restoration of maximum physical function of children with special/needs. The Physiotherapy Unit includes Exercise Therapy, Electro Therapy and Occupational Therapy equipments. This Unit provides services to clients with Developmental Disabilities, Orthopedic and Neurological Disabilities. This unit provides services to improve functions and develop activities of daily living. Parents of children are encouraged for regular follow up and consultation.

Audiology and Speech Pathology Services

This Unit Provides services to children with mental retardation and associated conditions related to hearing and functional as well as organic speech defects and disorders. A detailed assessment is got done followed by a comprehensive speech and language training programme.

Occupational Therapy Services

Occupational Therapy services are imparted to special children with the objective to promote and maintain health, and to prevent disability. The main focus of the therapy is to make the children improve independent activities of daily living and occupational calibre. The combined effort of Physiotherapy and occupation intervention can improve the quality of life of children.

Sports Facilities

Sports training provided by trained specialized trainers for indoor & outdoor games. The main objective of this unit is to make the child musculoskeletally fit & to train them in various sports according to body stature after a complete medical checkup & fitness testing camps. The better performance by children opens the doors of success in their specific sports field even at international level. Various competitions at district /state national level have been organized for our athletes.

With an objective to expose and provide children with mental retardation a platform to interact with the community and society at large, the children are taken for local sight seeing and to places of interest outside the city. Such programmes are conducted in a way that students are able to take maximum benefits.

Co-curricular
Programme of training in Music & Dance

This helps to improve their level of coordination, speech development and body movements. Students have shown remarkable development. In addition, sensing the need of nurturing the talents of gifted special children, special training sessions are organized in Music and Dance. Arpan has a section having all the required equipments and audio-visual aids. Children have performed and demonstrated their capabilities on various occasions.

Programme of training in Art & Craft skils

This Unit Provides services to children with mental retardation and associated conditions related to hearing and functional as well as organic speech defects and disorders. A detailed assessment is got done followed by a comprehensive speech and language training programme.

Occupational Therapy Services

Trainng in art & craft activities is provided by special trainer and this fulfill our objective of improving perceptual, cognitine, co-ordination & recreational skills of the students. The students goin confidence and this further helps in developing skill for vocational training.

Residential Facilities

Residential facilities were started on 11th Aug. 2009. Free of cost residential facilities are provided to special children belong to SC categories and BPL families. However CWSN belong to other categories are also enrolled & nominal charges are taken on the basis of annual income of their parents. At present, 45 special children are provided residential care facilities under the supervision of dedicated foster care mothers and hostel warden. Nutritious & balanced diet as per menu are provided to them in the hostel. Catering facilities to provide nutritious & balanced diet to CWSN have been outsourced by the institute. Cleanliness & proper hygiene in the canteen & dining halls are ensured by a committee constituted for this purpose. Recreational facilities i.e. T.V. programmes, music and others entertainment as well as sports facilities are available in the hostel for them.

Parents Empowerment
Programme of Parent Support Group through Parents Association

This unit plays a very important role in combined team work of rehabilitation of such children. The parents of such children are organised to form a parent association. Through this association parents are made aware about various meetings & counselling programme being organized from time to time. There is a direct communication of such association to the administration which solves the basic problems of their cases.

Programme of Parents/Group Meetings

This unit works with the motive of counselling. Parents are invited before & after completion of vacations like summer, winter to get all the home based program of different refrensive area of their ward. They intimate their wards class teacher as well as clinical staff members & get respective advises according to need. They are always free to suggest their suggestions and doubts. Since Arpan Institute has been working with an objective to empower parents and family members of children with mental retardation. In this meeting parents parents of children with mental retardation & members of the staff of Arpan interact in a group where in problems related to training & management of children are discussed and decided.

Transport Facilities

Most of the CWSN enrolled in the institute belong to rural setup for which transport facilities are available to pick up & drop them at their door steps under the supervision of bus boy & supporting staff. Children staying in the city are also benefited by availing such conveyance. Two 50 seaters buses are hired for this purpose.

Clinical Services
Programme of Diagnostic Clinical Services for cases (O.P.D)

Among all special services provided for rehabilitation Program of such children, clinical diagnostic services comes at top for every case. In the first step of getting admission in institute, a complete medical check up is taken up to know all physiological , psychological as well as musculokeletal aspects. This step also prevents the metastasis of communicable diseases. Fitness testing shows a path of their level of recovery & prognosis is made.

Programme of Case Conference

Since children with mental retardation require multidisciplinary intervention, hence it has been planned to conduct a case meeting on every Saturday. Concerned special educator/class incharge act as coordinator & details of particular case are presented by him/her. All the other professionals like Physiotherapist, occupational therapist, speech therapist, Psychologist present their reports like diagnosis, present level, goals and objectives etc.
