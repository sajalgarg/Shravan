About Us

SHRAVAN is the sole institute in North India run by the Red Cross Society with the objective to provide rehablitation and child care facilities to adult mentally challenged persons above 18 years of age. The foundation stone of SHRAVAN was laid down by Ch. Om Prakash Chautala, then Chief Minister of Haryana on 31st August 2002. This has been made possible by the pain-taking efforts of Shri AK Yadav, then IAS,Deputy Commisioner cum President, Distt. Red Cross Society, Rohtak and the blessings of the Hon. Chief Minister.The centre provide facilities to 50 adult mentally challenged students.

President's Message

In order to provide quality service to Persons with disabilities especially children & associated disabilities and their mainstreaming in the community, SIRTAR has been established in july 2008 an an autonomous body by the Deptt. Of Social Justice & Empowerment, Govt of Haryana within a spam of two years, institute has taken its momentum in the appropriate direction to achieve its objectives. Effective teaching strategies are implemented by qualified rehabilitation personnel for strengthening the various deficit skill areas i.e. Special Education, Pre-vocational Training, self help skills, functional academics etc. Residential facilities for Children with special needs by the institute are one of the best in the state of Haryana. Manpower training programmes in the field of special education and disability rehabilitation at Degree as per as Post Graduate levels through regular mode imparted by this institute ensures excellent grooming of trainees in order to make them proficient personal so that quality services could be provided to (Person with disibilisation) as it is one of the objectives of institute. I wish success to each one working for this Institute.
Dr. Amit Agarwal, I.A.S.,Deputy Commissioner Rohtak President

Facilities

Library
The school aims at all round development of a child's personality by providing education of very high quality, an atmosphere that is friendly and relaxed and a setting that furnishes all the necessary tools of learning. An extensive library has been painstakingly put together to attain this objective and boasts of a wide collection of books, educational films and music. A modern library stacked with a large collection of books of different spectrum, journals, periodicals, including many rare manuals is made accessible to facilitate the scholastic development and achievements of students. Besides serving the conventional purposes, the Library is also a 'resource centre' especially catering to the future career needs of the young learners. The cosmic library provides the students with the latest books being offered in the market.

Tranport
Safety of our students is our prime concern. Thus, our transport facility is fully equipped to provide the safest & the most convenient transport solution. The routes of the school buses are drawn up based on convenience and demand while ensuring that no route is overly long. Parents should consult the School’s transport in-charge for necessary details.

Hostel
The Institute has limited hostel facility which is available only for children residing out side Rohtak city. Hostel stay is valid till the students attain the age of 18 years. Arpan has three hostels having facilities for 10 children each. Boys and girls hostels are separate . Hostlers are provided home like facilities "A home away from home" Arpan Institute is spread over an area of 8949 SQ yards which has three hostel (each having stay facilities for 10 children), One Play Ground and green lawns etc.
